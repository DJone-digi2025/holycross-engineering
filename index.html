About the Institution

Where Ambition Meets
Opportunity

Holycross Engineering College provides a conducive environment for intellectual stimulation through a holistic learning experience.

Our focus is to provide our students with unlimited opportunities in education and personal development. Our institution stands for academic excellence and scientific inquiry. To achieve this, our campus has been designed to meet all the requirements of the students in their quest for knowledge.

We provide well furnished modern laboratories and updated libraries. We also have comfortable hostels and transport facilities for the students to make their education more accessible.

Our Vision

Vision

  • To serve the young minds by providing Quality Professional education in Engineering and Technology.
  • To create a global village wherein our students serves as pioneering torch bearers of technological expertise and professional excellence.
  • To generate a perennial stream of technically skilled youth, making significant contributions to the growth and development of our country.
  • To serve the community by providing quality and technical education to aspiring youths, especially those from the marginalized sections of society.
  • To evolve as a world class centre of technical excellence and also play a key role in creating a more equitable and enlightened social order.

Our Mission

To render the quality professional education through a Learner – Centric approach to empower the students in professional ethics, social responsibilities and enabling the students to meet the challenges in the field of Engineering and Technology.
