Admissions 2025–26

A clear, transparent process — no hidden steps. Here's exactly how to secure your seat.

Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

Meeting the minimum? Great. We're looking for motivated learners, not just scores.

📋

Academic Minimum

  • ✓ Class 12 / HSC passed
  • ✓ Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • ✓ Minimum 45% aggregate (40% for SC/ST)
  • ✓ TNEA / JEE / KEAM rank holders preferred
🧪

Lateral Entry (B.E.)

  • ✓ Diploma holders — 3 year program
  • ✓ B.Sc. mathematics graduates
  • ✓ Direct 2nd year admission
  • ✓ TNEAMAT rank required
🌍

NRI / Management Quota

  • ✓ 15% seats under management quota
  • ✓ NRI/PIO/OCI candidates welcome
  • ✓ Minimum 55% in qualifying exam
  • ✓ Direct counselling — no entrance required
Process

How to Apply

Five straightforward steps. Average completion time: 15 minutes.

Step 1
Fill Online Application
Complete the form below with your academic and personal details. Application fee: ₹500.
Step 2
Upload Documents
Class 10 & 12 marksheets, transfer certificate, ID proof (Aadhaar/Passport). All in PDF/JPG format.
Step 3
Counselling & Interview
Shortlisted candidates are invited for an in-person or virtual counselling session (30 mins).
Step 4
Allotment & Fee Payment
Accept your seat offer and pay the first semester fee to confirm enrollment.
Step 5
Report to Campus
Orientation starts July 28, 2025. Welcome to Holycross Engineering College!

Scholarships

We Invest in Talent

Over ₹1.2 Cr distributed in scholarships annually. Ambition shouldn't have a price tag.

🥇

Merit Scholarship

100%

Full tuition waiver for students scoring 90%+ in Class 12 HSC boards. Renewable annually based on CGPA ≥ 8.5.

🎖️

High Achiever

50%

50% tuition concession for students scoring 80–89% in boards. Renewable with CGPA ≥ 7.5.

🤲

Need-Based Aid

Up to 75%

Financial hardship grant. Family income ≤ ₹3 LPA. Requires income certificate and interview.
