FAQ

Common Questions

What are the admission requirements for B.E. programs? +

You must have completed Class 12 (HSC) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics with a minimum 45% aggregate. SC/ST candidates qualify at 40%. TNEA counselling rank is the primary selection criterion.

Is hostel accommodation mandatory? +

No, hostel is optional. Students from Trichirappalli and nearby areas typically commute. However, hostel significantly enriches the college experience, and we recommend it for outstation students.

How do scholarships work? +

We offer merit-based (up to 100% tuition waiver) and need-based scholarships. Merit scholarships are awarded at admission based on board scores and renewed annually based on CGPA. Need-based grants require income certificates and a brief interview.

Can I apply online, or must I visit campus? +

The entire application process — from submission to counselling — can be completed online. A campus visit is only required at the time of physical document verification and enrollment confirmation.

What is the nearest railway station / airport? +

Trichirappalli Junction Railway Station is 12 km from campus (approx. 25 min). Trichy International Airport is 8 km (approx. 15 min). College buses run from both points during term time.
