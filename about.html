Our Story, Our Purpose

Holycross Engineering College has been committed to providing quality technical education and fostering academic excellence.

Our Journey

From a Vision to a Leading Institution

Holycross Engineering College was established in 1999 with a single, uncompromising belief: engineering education should produce engineers who can actually engineer. Not just pass exams — but solve real problems, lead real teams, and build real things.

Over the years, the institution has grown steadily, offering multiple engineering programs and developing infrastructure to support academic and practical learning.

The college is affiliated to Anna University and approved by AICTE. It focuses on delivering quality education, research opportunities, and overall student development.
